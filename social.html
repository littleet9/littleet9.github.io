Volunteer

Camp Carefree is a 6-week overnight summer camp for kids with chronic illnesses. Since my freshmen year of high school, I have volunteered my summer at Camp Carefree. It truly is the greatest place on earth and is very dear to my heart. While volunteering at Camp Carefree I have been a counselor, staff member, and supervisor. Even though camp was canceled this past summer because of COVID, I am still very much involved with camp and the kids with creating online videos for Camp Carefree's Facebook and Youtube channel.
